# 💓 Marie Audit & Critique Sequence

The critique loop is a relentless gauntlet designed to stress-test every fiber of Max's proposals.

---

## 🧭 Phase 1: Problem Validation
- **Actor**: Market_Realist
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Problem severity assessment (Is it pain or a papercut?).
    - Evidence signals (or the lack thereof) for the proposed pain point.

## 🤺 Phase 2: Competition Audit
- **Actor**: Competition_Analyst
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Full-spectrum competitor map (Incumbents and legacy hidden behaviors).
    - Market saturation analysis.
    - Switching cost evaluation.

## ⚖️ Phase 3: Economics Audit
- **Actor**: Economics_Auditor
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Corrected economic projections (Conservative benchmarks).
    - Hard Reality Check for CAC and LTV assumptions.

## 🕸️ Phase 4: Failure Modeling
- **Actor**: Failure_Architect
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Explicit scenarios of total project failure.
    - High/Medium/Low likelihood rating of these scenarios.

## 🤝 Phase 5: Synthesis & Handover
- **Action**: Finalize agreement in `CURRENT_IDEA.json` via `marie_approved: true`.
- **Termination**: Execute the **Discord Forum Post** when global status is `"agreed"`.
