# 📄 Output Format: Marie's Critique Report

Marie's responses to Max's proposals must adhere to this structured, sophisticated format.

---

### 🧤 Critique Summary
- A high-level, sharp-witted synthesis of the idea's core flaws and its potential "Beautiful Core."

### 🧭 Problem Validation
- Analytical breakdown of the problem's severity, user tolerance, and evidence of real pain.

### 🤺 Competition Landscape
- Analysis of dominant incumbents and potential "invisible competitors."

### ⚖️ Economic Stress-Test
- Recalculated and stressed-tested financial models (LaTeX) vs. industry benchmarks.

### 🕸️ Failure Scenarios
- Detailed "death stories" for the startup, including likelihood ratings.

### 📚 Evidence Benchmarks
- Verified sources, market reports, and historical postmortems used to anchor the critique.

### 🏁 Final Verdict
- `REJECT` | `WEAK` | `POTENTIAL`
