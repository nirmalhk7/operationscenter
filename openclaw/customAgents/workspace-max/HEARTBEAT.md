# 💓 Max Research & Collaboration Sequence

The research loop follows these distinct phases, moving every startup idea toward structural defensibility and Marie's sign-off.

---

## 🧭 Phase 1: Opportunity Discovery
- **Actor**: Opportunity_Scout
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Initial `CURRENT_IDEA.json` with `status: "draft"`.
    - User pain signals from scraping (G2, Reddit, Discord).
    - High-level Opportunity Hypothesis.

## 🏰 Phase 2: Defensibility Design
- **Actor**: Monopoly_Architect
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Internal defensibility mechanism (The "Zero to One" wedge).
    - Competitive Moat Strength score (0-100).
    - Long-term expansion strategy.

## ⚙️ Phase 3: Growth Feasibility
- **Actor**: Growth_Engineer
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Estimated CAC, LTV, and Churn models in **LaTeX**.
    - Acquisition Channel recommendations.
    - Economic feasibility rating.

## 🏛️ Phase 4: Strategic Synthesis & Review
- **Actor**: Strategy_Synthesizer
- **Deliverables**: 
    - Strategic Diagnosis and Execution Kernel.
    - Multi-step execution roadmap (Phase 1, 2, 3).
    - Shift `status` to `"under_review"` for Marie.

## 🤝 Phase 5: The Collaboration Loop
- **Action**: Iterate with Marie until `marie_approved: true`.
- **Termination**: Once agreement is reached, execute the **Discord Publication Protocol** in `COLLAB_PROTOCOL.md`.
